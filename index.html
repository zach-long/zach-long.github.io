About

Greetings, and welcome to my portfolio. I am a web developer based out of Harrisburg, PA, with a passion for writing code that is, simply put, really great.

I consider myself a very proactive learner, always finding myself wanting to understand everything more deeply than I currently do. This has been an incredible boon as a developer because, while languages and computer science are based around some primary defining principles, the idiosyncrasies of individual languages lend themselves to requiring a great depth of knowledge before attaining mastery.
I find that there's always more to understand, and more technology to keep up with.

Perhaps one of my favorite aspects of programming/development is optimization, through all aspects of development. I enjoy crafting a plan and 'writing the blueprints' before starting a project because then (hopefully) I can sit down and write the best code. Likewise, I enjoy looking through after and refactoring to make the finished product faster, cleaner, and more maintainable.

I'm passionate about a job done well.
